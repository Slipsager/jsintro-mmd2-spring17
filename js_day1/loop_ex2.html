Multiplication Tables

  Write a for loop that will iterate from 0 to 10.
  For each iteration of the for loop,
  it will multiply the number by 9 and log the result

  (e.g. "2 * 9 = 18").

  Bonus:
  Use a nested (for loop in for loop) for loop to show the tables for every multiplier from 1 to 10
   (100 results total).


 0 * 9 = 0
 1 * 9 = 9
 2 * 9 = 18

 4 * 9 = 36
 5 * 9 = 45
